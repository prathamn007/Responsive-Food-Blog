Idli Vada Sambar

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup toor dal
  • 1 medium sized knol khol (kohlrab/ turnip chopped
  • 1 medium sized onion chopped
  • 2 to matoes chopped
  • 1/4 inch ginger finely chopped
  • 1 1/2 to 2 tbsp sambar powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon jaggery optional
  • Salt to taste

Method

  • Wash and rinse the dal well till the water runs clear Add it to the cooker along with 2 cups water.
  • Add onion, tomatoes, ginger, chopped turnip (or any veggie of your choice),sambar powder ,turmeric and salt to the cooker.Give them a good mix and pressure cook for 2 whistles
  • Once cooled ,open the lid and cook in medium flame. Add finely chopped coriander leaves and a tsp of jaggery.
  • Heat ghee in a small pan.Once hot add mustard seeds When they pop and in the rest of the tempering ingredients one by one and finally add the tempering to the boiling sambar.
  • Cover with the lid immediately so that the sambar soaks in all the flavors of the tempering and switch off the stove.
  • Let it rest for 2 minutes before serving.Hot sambar for idlis is now ready to be served!
